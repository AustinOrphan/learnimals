About Learnimals

Get to know our story, mission, and the team behind the animal friends

Our Mission

At Learnimals, we believe that learning should be a joyful adventure. Our mission is to create a fun, engaging, and safe digital environment where children can explore educational concepts through colorful animal friends who make learning exciting and accessible.

We combine playful design with educational principles to create games and activities that help children develop critical skills in math, science, reading, and art—all while having fun with their animal companions.

Our Story

2023

The idea for Learnimals was born when a group of educators wanted to create more engaging online learning resources for children.

2024

Our team of designers, developers, and educational experts came together to create the first version of Learnimals.

2025

Learnimals launched with our four core subjects and animal educators, reaching thousands of children worldwide.

Future

We continue to grow and develop new games, activities, and educational resources to make learning even more fun!

Educational Philosophy

Learnimals is built on these core educational principles:

  • Learning through play - Children learn best when they're having fun
  • Positive reinforcement - Encouragement and rewards boost confidence and motivation
  • Age-appropriate content - All activities are designed for the developmental needs of young learners
  • Multisensory learning - Engaging multiple senses helps children better understand and remember concepts
  • Inclusive design - Our games are made to be accessible for children of all abilities

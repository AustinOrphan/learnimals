👩‍🏫 For grown-ups: what this practises

Asteroid Math is quick-fire mental-arithmetic practice. Each round poses an addition, subtraction, or multiplication problem and asks the player to pick the correct answer from several floating options under light time pressure — building fluency, number sense, and the ability to reject plausible-but-wrong answers. Difficulty scales automatically: later levels use bigger numbers, more decoys, and a tighter timer.

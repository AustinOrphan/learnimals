🦜 Sky's Ecosystem Explorer

Build balanced ecosystems by placing species on the grid!

🎯 How to Play

  • Click species in the bottom palette
  • Click on the grid to place them
  • Create a balanced ecosystem
  • Watch the health bar turn green!

🌱 Available Species

Grass (Producer)
Rabbit (Herbivore)
Fox (Carnivore)

  • Food chains & energy flow
  • Species interdependence
  • Ecosystem balance
  • Environmental science

Sky's Ecosystem Explorer

Help me build balanced ecosystems! Drag species from the palette below to create thriving habitats.

🎯 Game Goals

  • Build a balanced ecosystem
  • Place producers, herbivores, and carnivores
  • Watch the ecosystem health bar
  • Green = healthy, Red = unbalanced

🌱 Species Types

🌱 Producers (Plants)
🐰 Herbivores (Plant-eaters)
🦊 Carnivores (Meat-eaters)

📚 Learning Focus

  • Food chains and energy flow
  • Species interdependence
  • Ecosystem balance
  • Habitat requirements

How to Play Sky's Ecosystem Explorer

1
Select Species: Click on species in the bottom palette or drag them directly
2
Place Species: Click on the grid or drag species to place them
3
Balance Ecosystem: Watch the health bar - aim for green (balanced)
4
Level Up: Complete levels by maintaining balance with 5+ species

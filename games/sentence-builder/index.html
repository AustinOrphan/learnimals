👩‍🏫 For grown-ups: what this practises

Bella's Sentence Builder develops grammar and reading skills by arranging words into correct sentences, reinforcing word order, capitalisation, and punctuation.

📖 What You'll Learn

Grammar Skills: Understanding parts of speech and sentence structure
Reading Comprehension: Building meaning through word order
Vocabulary: Learning new words and their meanings
Language Patterns: Recognizing common sentence structures

🌟 Reading Tips from Bella

  • Start sentences with capital letters
  • Listen to how sentences sound when you read them
  • Think about who or what the sentence is about
  • Notice how describing words make sentences more interesting

🎨 Amazing Color Facts
  • Primary colors cannot be made by mixing other colors
  • Secondary colors are made by mixing two primary colors
  • Colors can be warm (like fire) or cool (like water)
  • The color wheel helps artists choose harmonious colors

🌟 Artist Tips from Aria

  • Red, Blue, and Yellow are the primary colors
  • Mix primary colors to create secondary colors
  • Warm colors (reds, oranges, yellows) feel energetic
  • Cool colors (blues, greens, purples) feel calm
  • Opposite colors on the wheel create strong contrast

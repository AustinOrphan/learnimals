# Learnimals Educational Game Development Framework Roadmap

## Executive Summary

This roadmap outlines the strategic evolution of the Learnimals Educational Game Development Framework from 2024 through 2027. Building on the success of Story Safari and informed by educational technology research, this framework will establish Learnimals as the premier platform for accessible, effective educational gaming.

## Vision Statement

**"To create the world's most accessible, pedagogically sound, and engaging educational game development framework that empowers educators and delights learners while maintaining the highest standards of privacy, safety, and inclusion."**

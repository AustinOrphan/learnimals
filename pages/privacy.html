Privacy Policy

The short version: Learnimals runs entirely in your browser and does not collect your personal data.

The Short Version

Learnimals is a set of educational games that run entirely in your web browser. There is no account to create, no login, and no server that stores your information. Your progress stays on your own device, and nothing you do in the games is sent to us — because there is no "us" server to send it to.

What We Store (On Your Device)

To make the games work — remembering your scores, your chosen theme, and your progress — Learnimals saves a small amount of information in your browser's local storage. This includes things like:

  • Game scores, levels reached, and activity progress
  • Your display preferences, such as the color theme
  • Any nickname or avatar you set up in the profile area

This information lives only in your browser on your device. It is not transmitted to Learnimals or shared with anyone. You can erase all of it at any time by clearing your browser's site data for this site.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not ask children for names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personal details.
  • We do not run advertising or sell data.
  • We do not use third-party trackers to profile you across the web.
  • We do not require an account or log-in to play.

Hosting

The Learnimals site is served as static files from a web host. Like any website, the host may record basic, standard request information (such as IP address and browser type) in its own server logs for security and reliability. That logging is handled by the host, not by Learnimals, and is not combined with anything you do inside the games.

Children's Privacy

Learnimals is designed for children and is built to be safe by default: because it collects no personal information and requires no account, there is nothing for us to gather or store about a child. We encourage parents and guardians to explore the games alongside their children.

Questions

If you have questions about this policy, please reach out through the Contact page. This policy may be updated as the project evolves; the latest version always lives on this page.
